1 Overview

This document presents several wrapper functions to deal with a ggplot2 object.

library("SciencesPo", quietly = TRUE)

3 Themes

Graph() + theme_darkside()

4 Title

4.1 Align title right

Align ggplot2 title to the right.

Graph() + align_title_right()

4.2 Align title left

Align ggplot2 title to the left.

Graph() + align_title_left()

4.3 Remove ggplot2 title

Do not show plot title.

Graph() + no_title()

5 Plot Axes

5.1 Remove ggplot2 x-axis

Remove the x axis from a ggplot2 including the axis lines, titles, labels and ticks.

Graph() + no_x_axis()

5.2 Remove ggplot2 y-axis

Remove the y axis from a ggplot2 including the axis lines, titles, labels and ticks.

Graph() + no_y_axis()

5.3 Remove ggplot2 Axes

Remove both axes from a ggplot2 including the axes lines, titles, labels and ticks.

Graph() + no_axes()

5.4 Remove ggplot2 x-axis line

Do not show x-axis line.

Graph() + no_x_line()

5.5 Remove ggplot2 y-axis line

Do not show y-axis line.

Graph() + no_x_line()

5.6 Remove ggplot2 x-axis text/labels

Do not show x-axis text/labels

Graph() + no_x_text()

5.7 Remove ggplot2 y-axis text/labels

Do not show y-axis text/labels.

Graph() + no_y_text()

5.8 Remove ggplot2 x-axis tick marks

Do not show tick marks for x-axis.

Graph() + no_x_ticks()

5.9 Remove ggplot2 y-axis tick marks

Do not show tick marks for y-axis.

Graph() + no_y_ticks()

5.10 Remove ggplot2 tick marks

Do not show tick marks for all axes.

Graph() + no_ticks()

5.11 Remove ggplot2 x-axis titles

Do not show x-axis title.

Graph() + no_x_title()

5.12 Remove ggplot2 y-axis titles

Do not show y-axis title.

Graph() + no_y_title()

5.13 Remove ggplot2 axes titles

Do not show titles for all axes.

Graph() + no_axes_titles()

5.14 Rotate ggplot2 x-axis labels

Rotate the x-axis labels/text by a given angle.

Graph() + rotate_x_text(90)

5.15 Rotate ggplot2 y-axis labels

Rotate the y-axis labels/text by a given angle.

Graph() + rotate_y_text(65)

5.16 Rotate ggplot2 axes labels

Rotate both axes labels by a given angle.

Graph() + rotate_axes_text(45)

6 Plot Gridlines

6.1 Remove vertical gridlines

Remove all major and minor vertical gridlines from a ggplot2 plot or theme.

Graph() + no_x_gridlines()

6.2 Remove horizontal gridlines

Remove all major and minor horizontal gridlines from a ggplot2 plot or theme.

Graph() + no_y_gridlines()

6.3 Remove all gridlines

Remove all vertical and horizontal gridlines (major and minor) from a ggplot2 plot or theme.

Graph() + no_gridlines()

6.4 Remove major x gridlines

Do not show major gridlines for x-axis.

Graph() + no_major_x_gridlines()

6.5 Remove major y gridlines

Do not show major gridlines for y-axis.

Graph() + no_major_y_gridlines()

6.6 Remove major gridlines

Do not show major gridlines.

Graph() + no_major_gridlines()

6.7 Remove minor vertical gridlines

Do not show minor gridlines for x-axis.

Graph() + no_minor_x_gridlines()

6.8 Remove minor horizontal gridlines

Do not show minor gridlines for y-axis.

Graph() + no_minor_y_gridlines()

6.9 Remove minor gridlines

Do not show minor gridlines.

Graph() + no_minor_gridlines()

7 Legend

7.1 Remove ggplot2 legend

Remove the legend from a ggplot2 plot.

Graph() + no_legend()

7.2 Remove ggplot2 legend title

Remove the legend title from a ggplot2 plot.

Graph() + no_legend_title()

7.3 Legend Position

Move legend to given location. The location of the legend is top, bottom, right, left or none.

Graph() + move_legend('bottom')

7.4 Legend to the bottom

Move a ggplot2 legend to the bottom of the plot.

Graph() + legend_bottom()

7.5 Legend to the top

Move a ggplot2 legend to the top of the plot.

Graph() + legend_top()

7.6 Legend to the left

Move a ggplot2 legend to the left of the plot.

Graph() + legend_left()

7.7 Legend to the right

Move a ggplot2 legend to the right of the plot.

Graph() + legend_right()
